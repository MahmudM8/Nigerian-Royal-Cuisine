Our Culinary Heritage

"Our kitchen honors the land, the people, and the joy of gathering."

Founded by Chef Adebayo Olawale, Royal Nigerian Cuisine brings generations of culinary wisdom to Lagos' dining scene. Chef Adebayo's journey began in his grandmother's kitchen in Ibadan, where he learned the sacred traditions of Yoruba cuisine.

Today, we source ingredients from local farmers and artisans across Nigeria's diverse regions, celebrating each culture's unique flavors while elevating them with contemporary techniques.
